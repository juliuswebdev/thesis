Brief History

of Barangay Bahay Pare

  • Bahay Pare is a barangay in the city of Meycauayan, in the province of Bulacan. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 13,232. This represented 5.86% of the total population of Meycauayan.
  • The household population of Bahay Pare in the 2015 Census was 11,568 broken down into 2,851 households or an average of 4.06 members per household.
  • Bahay Pare is situated at approximately 14.7673, 121.0109, in the island of Luzon. Elevation at these coordinates is estimated at 55.4 meters or 181.8 feet above mean sea level.

    • Bahay Pare shares a common border with the following barangay(s):

  • Loma de Gato, Marilao, Bulacan
  • Barangay 175, Caloocan
  • Pajo, Meycauayan, Bulacan
  • Barangay 171, Caloocan
  • Bagbaguin, Meycauayan, Bulacan
  • Perez, Meycauayan, Bulacan
